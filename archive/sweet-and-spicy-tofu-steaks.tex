Add tofu and fry on one side for 3 minutes or until browned. Flip and fry on second side for 1 minute fewer.\item Add sauce mixture evenly over tofu steaks and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, flipping to coat before removing from pan.\end{enumerate}\end{document}
