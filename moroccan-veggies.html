Moroccan Veggies
Ingredients:
  • 1 box vegetable broth
  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 large yellow onion, coarsely chopped
  • 25 baby carrots
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube
  • 1 teas turmeric
  • 1/2 teas ground cloves
  • 1/2 teas cinnamon
  • 1/2 teas chili powder
  • 1/4 teas ground ginger
Directions:
  1. Pour vegetable broth into a large pan and whisk in seasonings.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a gentle boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low, and simmer (covered) until veggies are tender. Serve over couscous.
