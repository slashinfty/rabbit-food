Za'atar Spice
Ingredients:
  • 2 tbs toasted ground cumin
  • 2 tbs tosated ground coriander
  • 2 tbs ground sumac
  • 2 tbs dried thyme
  • 2 tbs toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teas kosher salt
  • 3/4 teas aleppo chili flakes
Directions:
  1. Mix spices together in a small bowl. Store in an airtight container.
